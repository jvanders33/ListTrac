Data & acknowledgements

ListTrac tracks contract status, never salary figures — the AFL does not disclose contract terms. Built with gratitude on data drawn from:

  • Champion Data / AFL — official AFL Player Ratings, AFL Fantasy scoring, and prospect & state-league statistics (Coates Talent League, U18 Championships, VFL, SANFL).
  • Rookie Me Central — junior playing history (WAFL, U16 championships and reserves grades).
  • AFLRATINGS — contract, trade and player-movement reporting.
  • Draftguru & AFL Tables — draft picks, trades and historical club lists.
  • Zero Hanger and AFL.com.au — free agency and contract status.
  • Squiggle — live ladder powering the projected draft order.
  • Wikipedia — club premiership and individual-medal honour rolls.
  • Individual clubs' official sites — verified contract announcements.

Club names, colours and guernsey representations are used for identification only. ListTrac is an independent project, not affiliated with or endorsed by the AFL or any club.
